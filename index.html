KNOW MORE ABOUT ME

Greetings from Abiral Subedi, Nepal! I have recently passed A level examination, meanwhile preparing for foreign undergraduate college application. I am addicted to computer and my passion for Computer Science and technology emerged when my father first bought me a computer while I was at grade 4. At that time, I had very little knowledge of programming and designing, so QBASIC was the only programming language and Microsoft Paint was my designing platform. I used to spend whole day by writing simple QBASIC codes and modifying them to get desired output, drawing rough surrounding pictures, and playing computer games. As I grew older, my sense of Computer Science has also matured and lately I have been using advanced software such as Adobe Illustrator, Python for designing and coding.

Similarly, music has always been a release from the stress and frustration I feel from a long day and an empowerment I require the next day. Though listening to music was my hobby since childhood, I started pursuing music as a passion at grade 11, where I began learning guitar and vocal. Music and Computer have been inseparable part of my life, with which I can enjoy every moment.

I LIVE OFF code, design and music.
